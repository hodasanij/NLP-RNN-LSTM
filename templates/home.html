Deep Learning App

Spam Detector For Email Messages

Spam detection is an NLP classification problem.
We employ deep learning; when they are strong at sequential input and have a memory,
they can keep track of previous words and orders.

Our project's purpose is to create a comprehensive and reliable model that can predict if a brief Email message is spam or non-spam.
